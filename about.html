About Me

I consider myself extremely fortunate due to my entrepreneurial journey. When I see my younger self, I think how lucky I am as a person without luck, it is never possible what I have done and what I have achieved till now.

My entrepreneurial journey started with an accident when I met a visually impaired student. In my opinion, everyone should at least try an entrepreneurial journey. In the previous three years, I have built Veeery from scratch. While building this startup I shifted my role from coder to a salesperson to a product guy.

These days I am focusing on my NGO(Dharnika Foundation). Dharnika started at the request of our visually impaired friends. I am so glad that they considered me worthy to do the work in the field of inclusive education and livelihood. At Dharnika I have learned about team building and leading them without authority.

While working on Veeery and Dharnika I met a lot of nice people, these people are my inspiration to continue doing these noble works.

I know that I am a nobody people don't know me by my name or face. I have not created much impact on society, but whatever the little work I have done, all credit goes to Kishore Sir for their guidance on every stage of my entrepreneurial journey.

I also want to say a big thanks to Rajeev Samuel and Nhung Nguyen, who are my mentors and helping me in my current learning process.
