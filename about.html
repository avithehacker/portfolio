About Me

I consider myself very fortunate due to my entrepreneurial journey. When I see my younger self, I think how lucky I am as a person. Without luck, it is never possible what I have done and what I have achieved till now.

My entrepreneurial journey started with an accident when I met a visually impaired student. While talking to him, I realized that we are living in 21 century where we are trying to solve every problem with technology. So why cannot we solve the education problem of visually impaired friends?

In my opinion, everyone should at least try an entrepreneurial journey in his life. In three years, I have built Veeery from scratch. While making Veeery, I shifted my role from coder to a salesperson to a product guy.

These days I am focusing on my NGO(Dharnika Foundation). Dharnika started at the request of our visually impaired friends. I am so glad that they considered me a worthy person and told me to work in the field of inclusive education and livelihood at Dharnika. In Dharnika, I have learned about team building and leading them without authority.

While working on Veeery and Dharnika, I met many good people. These people are my inspiration to continue doing these noble works.

I know I am a nobody people don't know me by my name or face. I have not created much impact on society, but whatever the little work I have done. All credit goes to Kishore Sir for their guidance on every stage of my entrepreneurial journey.

I also want to say a big thanks to Rajeev Samuel and Nhung Nguyen. My mentors for helping me in my current learning process.
